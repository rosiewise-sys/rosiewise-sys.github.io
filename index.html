Solhydra — Hydration Drink Brand

A bold landing page for Solhydra, an ionized low-sugar hydration drink designed for everyday Africans — workers, professionals, and athletes. Built during a hackathon, the design features a dark navy identity, strong product photography, a competitor comparison table, and an ingredient breakdown section. The full Figma design includes a scrolling marquee ticker strip below the hero section that highlights key brand values — this element was not carried into the live Vercel build.

A bit about me

I'm Rosemary — a UI/UX designer and WordPress support specialist who cares deeply about how things work and how they feel to use.

My journey into WordPress was fast by necessity: I joined a hackathon, had to set up and ship a real site, and I did. What I discovered is that I love the process of making sure everything works — from plugin configuration to payment flows to a smooth user experience.

I'm growing into WordPress support with genuine curiosity. I don't just complete tasks — I go back and make them better, which is exactly what I did with Solhydra after the hackathon ended.

Currently looking for opportunities where I can grow fast, support users meaningfully, and keep building.
